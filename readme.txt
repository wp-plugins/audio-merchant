* Multiple pricing formats! You can sell your audio lease, exclusive, one-time download fee or give them away for free download, it’s up to you! Perfect for artists, producers and musicians!
* Supports multiple currencies!
* Supports multiple languages! Translation ready!
* Versatile HTML5 player built-in to allow you to sell or showcase your audio!
* Customizable look! Use CSS and HTML to customize the HTML5 player to your needs!

* Multiple HTML5 players on a page!
* Cover photo support! Upload a custom cover photo for your audio!

* Preview file capability built-in in case you want to protect your full quality audio file from listeners!

== Installation ==

1. Download the Audio Merchant WordPress plugin.
2. Next, login to your WordPress Admin (/wp-admin/) and install the Audio Merchant plugin you downloaded in step 1 onto your WordPress website, by going to “Plugins -> Add New -> Upload Plugin” from the left hand menu on your WordPress Admin.
3. After installing the plugin successfully, please ensure you activate the plugin by going to “Plugins -> Installed Plugins”
4. Congratulations! You are done installing the Audio Merchant plugin onto your website! Next you should start uploading your audio tracks and place a Audio Merchant HTML5 audio player widget onto the frontend of your website by following the steps listed below.

Please see our documentation for further information at:
http://www.myaudiomerchant.com/documentation/

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I disable the selling feature and only showcase my audio files in the HTML5 player? =

Yes, if you don't provide the Audio Merchant plugin with a receiving PayPal address then it will automatically disable the sell option and your listeners will only be able to listen to your audio.

= What are the system requirements? =

1. PHP 5.3.0 or greator.
2. WordPress 3.6.0 or greator.
